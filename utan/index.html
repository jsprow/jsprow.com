About

U Tan is Southwest Michigan's largest tanning facility, featuring 30 tanning units, a clean facility, and great customer service. U Tan has been selected as a Top 250 Tanning Facility in the United States in both 2008 and 2009. We continually strive to bring the top technology in tanning to our customers.

  • Mystic Tan The 30 second UV-free sunless tan--an instant tan that's great for any special occasion (wedding, prom, party, etc.)
  • Hydro Massage Bed A great 30 minute warm-water massage--recommended by the Chiropractic Industry
  • Medium Pressure VHR Beds For that quick 12-minute tan
  • Regular Beds For your base tan
  • No Appointments at U Tan See our hours of operation below
  • Great Customer Service

Home of the $4.00 tan, U Tan's prices and service will never be beaten!
